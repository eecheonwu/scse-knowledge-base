Dr. Emmanuel Chinyere Echeonwu

Researcher & Software Engineer — AI-native & Agentic Software Engineering

Dr. Emmanuel C. Echeonwu is a researcher and software engineer focused on AI-native software development and agentic software engineering. He developed the SSOT-Centric Agentic Software Engineering Framework (SSOT-ASEF) and works across AI, geospatial intelligence, and machine learning to produce reproducible, architecture-first solutions.

Education: Ph.D. in Software Engineering (Nnamdi Azikiwe University); M.Sc. in Machine Intelligence (AIMS, Rwanda); M.Sc. in Geospatial & Mapping Sciences (University of Glasgow); B.Sc. Computer Science (University of Uyo).

Research: Agentic Software Engineering, AI-native architectures, SSOT-Centric Software Engineering, multi-agent systems, and knowledge preservation during software evolution. Work includes efficient deep-learning models for vegetation monitoring and retrieval-augmented approaches for domain-specific information access.

Recognized as a Google PhD Research Fellow (ML) and active in open research and tooling on GitHub.
