About SCSE

This initiative explores Single Source of Truth (SSOT)-centric approaches to software engineering. The SCSE knowledge base collects research notes, artifacts, experiments, validations, and practical guidance to advance reproducible, governable software systems.

The canonical repository for the initiative is the SCSE Knowledge Base on GitHub:

github.com/eecheonwu/scse-knowledge-base

Research Areas

  • SSOT design patterns for large-scale systems
  • Knowledge representation for engineering artifacts
  • Validation & reproducibility techniques
  • Evolution and governance of knowledge-centric systems
