# SSOT-Centric Software Engineering (SCSE) Research Monograph

> **The official Handbook for the SCSE Research Programme**

***

## Welcome

Welcome to the SCSE Research Handbook.

This handbook serves as the authoritative knowledge base governing the research, development, validation, implementation, and evolution of **SSOT-Centric Software Engineering (SCSE)**.

It is maintained as the Single Source of Truth (SSOT) for the entire research programme.

Unlike traditional documentation, the handbook is itself an implementation of SCSE principles. Every concept, architectural decision, experiment, publication, prototype, and software artifact developed within the programme is intended to trace back to this repository.

***

# Purpose

The handbook has four primary purposes.

- Preserve engineering knowledge.

- Coordinate research activities.

- Support reproducible research.

- Guide the development of the SCSE body of knowledge.

***

# Handbook Structure

The handbook is organized into twelve major sections.

## 1. Research Governance

Defines the vision, mission, philosophy, governance model, and roadmap of the SCSE programme.

***

## 2. Canonical Research Library

Maintains the curated literature supporting every concept developed within SCSE.

***

## 3. SCSE Body of Knowledge

Defines every concept, theory, framework, metric, and methodology.

***

## 4. Book Management

Tracks the development of the SCSE monograph.

***

## 5. Contribution Register

Maintains every original contribution proposed by the programme.

***

## 6. Research Traceability

Provides complete traceability from literature to theory, implementation, experiments, and publications.

***

## 7. Validation

Documents empirical studies, benchmarks, datasets, and evaluation methodologies.

***

## 8. Publication Pipeline

Tracks journal papers, conference papers, technical reports, and future books.

***

## 9. Prototype Development

Documents all software implementations produced by the programme.

***

## 10. Metrics

Defines the metrics used to evaluate SCSE, including STQE and future quality measures.

***

## 11. Glossary

Maintains the official vocabulary of SCSE.

***

## 12. Decision Log

Records significant research and architectural decisions throughout the evolution of the programme.

***
